Bean Salad

Only takes 10 minutes to make!

A delicious and hearty bean & avocado salad

Ingredients

  • Finely-diced Shallots: 3
  • One Can of Red Kidney Beans
  • One Can of Black Beans
  • One can of Corn
  • Olive Oil: 2 tbsp
  • Vinegar: 1 tbsp
  • Avocado: 1
  • Red Chili Powder

Steps

  1. Mix shallots, red chili powder, vinegar and olive oil together
  2. Add beans to bowl, add above mixture, stir well
  3. Right before eating, add one full avocado
