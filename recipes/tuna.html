Tuna Stew

My boyfriend's legendary 10-minute tuna stew

I'm not kidding, it only takes 5 minutes, is healthy, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Finely-diced Shallots: 3-4
  • Anchovies: 4-5 + oil
  • Kalamata Olives: 2 spoonfulls
  • Chili Pepper Flakes: up to you
  • Tuna: 1 can
  • Tomato (with chunks): 1 can
  • Lentils: 1 can

Steps

  1. Add aromatics: anchovies, shallots, olives, chili pepper flakes, cook on high heat until shallots are transparent
  2. Add Tuna, on high until tuna is cooked, stir often
  3. Add tomato, stir well
  4. Add lentils, place lid on pan and simmer on low heat for 3 minutes
