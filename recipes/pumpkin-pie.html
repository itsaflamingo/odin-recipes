Pumpkin Pie

Sourced from Bouchon Bakery

This is my favourite pie, sourced from one of my favourite chefs Thomas Keller's bakery, "Bouchon Bakery". It stands out from other pumpkin pies in its brown butter base and rich use of spices.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour - 2Tbs
  • Eggs - 2 (Room temp)
  • Yolk - 2
  • Dark Brown Sugar - 100g
  • Ground Cloves - 2 pinches
  • Ground Nutmeg - 1 pinch
  • Ground Allspice - 2 pinches
  • Cinnamon - 1/4 tsp
  • Buttermilk - 75g
  • Brown Butter - 2.5Tbs
  • Tart Shell - 1

Steps

  1. Whisk together the flour, eggs and yolks in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Slowly pour in the sugar and whisk together until fully combined with mixture. Do the same for the pumpkin, spices and buttermilk.
  3. Lastly, whisk in the butter until fully incorporated and smooth.
  4. Pour mixture into an 8″ x 1″ tart shell and bake at 300F for approx. 35 minutes, or until the center slightly jiggles. Cool completely before decorating with fresh whipped cream.
